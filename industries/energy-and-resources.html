      Empowering Energy's Future, Today

      Explore Our Work 

      • Turning future possibilities into today’s reality for energy companies. 

      Energy drives economic growth, employment, affordability of goods and services, climate stability and geopolitical balance. Industries, governments and citizens must navigate the complex balance of energy security, affordability and the transition to low-carbon sources. This balance is often disrupted by geopolitical conflicts, climate events and global crises.

      Organizations in the energy sector face an unprecedented level of complexity in making business and investment decisions while maintaining agility to adapt in an unpredictable environment. The transformative potential of generative AI and digital technologies provides the pathways to new levels of efficiency, resilience and sustainability. Our interdisciplinary approach combines deep domain expertise, scalable technical competency and transformational consulting to turn these future possibilities into today's reality.

      Our Accelerators

      Well Log Parser

      End-to-end ingestion workflow for digital well logs (LAS, LIS, DLIS) that includes parsing, data quality flagging, manifest ingestion and wellbore DDMS ingestion.

      Petrel to Cloud via Ocean API

      A connector between the Petrel desktop application and the cloud to access resources and perform computations that local machines can't.

      SEGY Cloud-Ready Library & API

      A library that scans the SEGY file, extracts the location of each trace in the file and uses an index to find and extract the desired portion of seismic data from cloud storage.

      DevOps Automation Scripts

      A collection of automation scripts for tasks in the OSDU platform such as assigning entitlements, managing data partitions, registering schemas and more.

      OSDU Manifest-Based Ingestion & Quality Check

      OSDU-specific data validation checks as part of the manifest-based ingestion workflow to customize validation and quality checks.

      DEPS for OSDU

      A service-based document processing platform for improving classic OCR and text extraction accuracy by using ML pre/post processors, analytics capabilities and more.

      AI/ML-Enabled Metadata Extraction

      OSDU R3 manifest-based ingestion enhancement, enabling the metadata extraction of well logs in various scan formats.

      Neon-Green

      A data mapper tool that allows a user to map data into the OSDU canonical JSON schemas from a variety of flat formats, supporting data transformation and reusable templates.

      Deltix Systematic Trading Platform

      A high-performance application ecosystem covering the whole spectrum of functionality for intelligent trading: from data acquisition through research and strategy development to simulated and production trading, with a full stack of risk controls.

      Deltix QuantOffice Energy

      QuantOffice Energy is an advanced systematic trading platform tailored specifically for energy markets. Our high-performance application ecosystem covers a set of well-integrated functionalities.

      InfoNgen®

      InfoNgen is a text analytics, sentiment analysis and semantic search solution that automatically finds actionable insights hidden in mountains of data. InfoNgen helps you leverage text to optimize decision science quickly and easily.
