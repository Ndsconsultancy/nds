      Building Tomorrow's MedTech Ecosystem

      Transformative MedTech solutions blend physical, digital and data platform products that work together seamlessly to enable better outcomes. We deliver results — from ideation to market release — across the complex health and medical ecosystem.

      We seamlessly build and test alongside clients and industry leaders, accelerating valuable experiments to secure early tech feasibility and strategic direction. Clients partner with us to validate data-enriched business opportunities as well as design, develop and commercialize medical devices, solutions and systems that are compliant with regulatory and quality requirements.

      For decades, our engineers, designers and strategists have collaborated end-to-end or at any point along the way with providers and patients where they work and where they live to drive the future of healthcare and MedTech.

      • Our Core MedTech
        Focus Areas

      Medical Ecosystem Strategy & Experience Design

       

      Medical Ecosystem Strategy & Experience Design

      We research, analyze and roadmap the best experience for providers and patients by leveraging our expert-driven product strategy process to increase market share and create opportunities to break through into new markets.

      Medical Devices

      Medical Devices

      Leveraging a highly-integrated multidisciplinary team of experts, we design and develop physical and digital devices with improved performance, usability and experience for more effective medical encounters.

      We bring our broad and deep expertise in automation, diagnostics, surgical devices, software as a medical device (SaMD), internet of medical things (IoMT) and wearables to integrated product development engagements with accelerated time to market.

      Learn More About Our Physical Product Development Services

      Patient & Provider Digital Applications

       

      Patient & Provider Digital Applications

      We create digital solutions that enable patient and physician engagement and drive better health outcomes.

      From omnichannel digital experience strategy to summative testing, we build digital therapeutics, biomarker and patient reported/patient important outcome monitoring (ePRO/ePIO), and practice management applications with seamless user interfaces.

      Health Data Platforms

      Health Data Platforms

      We develop modern and interoperable data environments from the edge to the cloud, enabling diagnostic and therapeutic breakthroughs for precision and personalized medicine.

      Our data experts build compliant data factories for clinical data integration at scale, content management for patient and physician support, and algorithms, analytics and machine learning solutions for optimization.

      Commercialization & Go To Market

       

      Commercialization & Go To Market

      We interweave our core design, development and testing capabilities in medical devices, software applications and digital platforms to create products that work together efficiently in compliance with regulatory standards as they’re transferred through manufacturing, commercialization and into the hands of users in the real world
