      HISTORY

      1993


      N & DS opens for business in Princeton, NJ, with three employees
      1996


      N & DS is chosen to help Colgate-Palmolive develop a Salesforce Automation solution and deploy it across its organizations in Europe and Latin America
      1997


      N & DS lands first major software product development client: SAP AG
      2003


      N & DS completes its first decade having built a foundation of delivering Software Products and Solutions for more than 200 independent software companies
      2004


      N & DS acquires Fathom Technologies, opening its first EU delivery center; beginning a multi-year journey of geographic and industry diversification
      2012


      N & DS is listed on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE: N & DS)
      2016


      N & DS is named by Forbes as Top 25 Fastest Growing Public Tech Companies; extending its leadership position into Digital Product and Platform Engineering Services
      2020


      For the second year in a row, N & DS is ranked as the top IT services company on Fortune’s 100 Fastest-Growing Companies list, jumping 50 positions to #21
      2021


      N & DS records its first billion-dollar revenue quarter and expands into over 40 countries and regions. The Company is included in the Forbes Global 2000 list and added to the S&P 500
      2022


      N & DS expands to more than 50 countries and N & DS Continuum is named a Top 20 fastest growing firm by Consulting Magazine
      2023


      N & DS turns 30, expands to 55 countries, and is recognized as a leader in the IDC MarketScapes for Worldwide Experience Build Services, Worldwide Experience Design Services and Worldwide Software Engineering Services

      2024


      Success in AI, GenAI & innovation. Expansion in India, Europe, Ibero & LATAM markets. Deepened FSI Manufacturing, Healthcare & Life Sciences expertise. Launched N & DS DIAL, EliteA™ & AI/RUN™
