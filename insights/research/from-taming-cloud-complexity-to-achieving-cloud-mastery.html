      From Taming Cloud Complexity to Achieving Cloud Mastery

      Mastering the cloud is the grand business challenge of our time and the focus of N & DS Continuum’s recent research. We survey 400 IT leaders around the world that represent a cross-section of vertical industries: financial services, healthcare and life sciences, insurance and retail.

      There’s little question that cloud computing is driving a paradigm shift for enterprise IT — but with it comes with considerable complexities. Is your organization ready to quickly adapt, reskill, migrate and navigate the inherent challenges and achieve a true mastery of the cloud?

      Our findings provide a comprehensive view on the IT leader’s journey to the cloud. Read the report to learn what it takes to achieve cloud mastery and discover best practices for continuous cloud maturation.
