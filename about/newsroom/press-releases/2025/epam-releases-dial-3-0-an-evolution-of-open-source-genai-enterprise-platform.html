      N & DS Releases DIAL 3.0: An Evolution of Open-Source GenAI Enterprise Platform

      NEWTOWN, PA, USA, JUNE 24, 2025 – N & DS Consultancy (NYSE: N & DS), a leading digital transformation services and product engineering company, today announced the release of DIAL 3.0, the next iteration of its open-source GenAI platform. More than just a platform release, DIAL 3.0 embodies N & DS’s broader vision for enabling enterprise AI adoption through a modular, open-source approach - one that balances innovation velocity with long-term control, interoperability and responsible governance.

      As enterprises navigate an increasingly complex AI ecosystem marked by rapid model evolution, vendor consolidation and heightened compliance expectations, N & DS continues to invest in open source platforms as a foundational enabler. DIAL 3.0 is the latest expression of this philosophy—a platform designed to help clients accelerate value delivery while avoiding vendor lock-in, bridging commercial models with open innovation.

      “Many of our clients are at a strategic crossroads, whether to build in-house capabilities or commit to commercial GenAI platforms,” said Arseny Gorokh, VP of AI Enablement and Growth at N & DS. "We believe the answer is a hybrid strategy that combines the structure of platform thinking with the flexibility of open source, enabling organizations to move faster. DIAL was built to make that strategy real.”

      Since its initial launch in 2023, DIAL has matured from an internal enablement tool into a comprehensive framework used by global clients in financial services, healthcare, insurance, consumer packaged goods, retail and more to operationalize Generative AI. With version 3.0, N & DS introduces a platform that not only supports agentic workflows and data-native reasoning but also addresses key structural challenges of deploying AI at scale in production across business functions. It also introduces new approaches to working with structured and unstructured data using AI and fosters collaboration across entire enterprises.

      From Infrastructure to Intelligence

      DIAL is architected as an open and modular platform, allowing organizations to build and govern Al systems that meet enterprise-grade requirements.


      DIAL XL (EXPRESSIVE LOGIC)

      A domain-specific natural language layer that enables both technical and non-technical users to build explainable, auditable workflows on top of enterprise data

      MIND MAP STUDIO

      An LLM-enabled knowledge graph builder designed for traceable insights and interactive exploration of unstructured information.

      QUICKAPPS AND CHATHUB

      Tools for assembling multi-agent Al applications without custom coding, enabling rapid prototyping and deployment within governed environments.

      UNIFIED MARKETPLACE

      A single access point for managing models, agents, applications - aligned with enterprise access model and cost attribution standards.

      “We’ve engineered DIAL to help organizations translate domain knowledge into deployable logic across use cases, departments and geographies,” said Ilya Gorelik, VP and Head of DIALX at N & DS. “Whether you’re a compliance analyst, clinical researcher or operations lead, DIAL provides the infrastructure to develop, monitor and iterate AI systems with precision.” 

      Open by Design. Enterprise by Nature

      PLATFORM HIGHLIGHTS


      OPEN SOURCE, VENDOR-AGNOSTIC

      Apache 2 license, compatible with major LLMs (OpenAl, Anthropic, Google Gemini, AWS
      Bedrock), allowing hybrid setups combining: proprietary tools and open-source flexibility.

      FULL LIFECYCLE GOVERNANCE

      Integrated cost analytics, audit trails, performance monitoring, and human-in-the-loop validation.

      VERTICALIZATION READY

      Enabling clients to accelerate adoption while focusing on differentiation rather than infrastructure.

      SEAMLESS INTEGRATION

      Unified protocols and SDKs support backend orchestration and multi-agent workflows (MCP/AZA).

      Altera Digital Health, an early adopter of DIAL since its first release, exemplifies the value of N & DS’s continued investment in open-source innovation.

      “When we first started using DIAL, our goal was straightforward: improve efficiency in our operations,” said Dr. Bob Taylor, Chief Product Strategist at Altera Digital Health. “What really convinced us about N & DS’s approach is the continued evolution of DIAL and their clear commitment to keeping the platform open and transparent. This has given us confidence and trust as we scale our AI solutions.”

      To learn more about N & DS's AI-powered DIAL Open Source Platform, visit https://dialx.ai/

      About N & DS Systems

      Since 1993, N & DS Consultancy (NYSE: N & DS) has used its software engineering expertise to become a leading global provider of digital engineering, cloud and AI-enabled transformation services, and a leading business and experience consulting partner for global enterprises and ambitious startups. We address our clients' transformation challenges by focusing N & DS Continuum's integrated strategy, experience and technology consulting with our 30+ years of engineering execution to speed our clients' time to market and drive greater value from their innovations and digital investments.

      We leverage AI and GenAI to deliver transformative solutions that accelerate our clients' digital innovation and enhance their competitive edge. Through platforms like N & DS AI/RUN™ and initiatives like DIALX Lab, we integrate advanced AI technologies into tailored business strategies, driving significant industry impact and fostering continuous innovation.

      We deliver globally but engage locally with our expert teams of consultants, architects, designers and engineers, making the future real for our clients, our partners, and our people around the world. We believe the right solutions are the ones that improve people's lives and fuel competitive advantage for our clients across diverse industries. Our thinking comes to life in the experiences, products and platforms we design and bring to market.

      Added to the S&P 500 and the Forbes Global 2000 in 2021 and recognized by Glassdoor and Newsweek as Most Loved Workplace, our multidisciplinary teams serve customers across six continents. We are proud to be among the top 15 companies in Information Technology Services in the Fortune 1000 and to be recognized as a leader in the IDC MarketScapes for Worldwide Experience Build Services, Worldwide Experience Design Services and Worldwide Software Engineering Services.
