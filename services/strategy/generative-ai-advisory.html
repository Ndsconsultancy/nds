      Generative
      AI Advisory

      Setting the course for your future as an AI-enabled enterprise.

      Explore Our Work

      A call to action for generative AI has been sounded. 

      The rapid adoption and acceleration of generative AI will be as disruptive as the launch of the ATM, internet, smart phone and electric vehicle. Businesses are bracing for impact.

      And there’s no time to waste. To safeguard the future of your business, it's crucial to adapt swiftly to the disruptive influence of emerging technologies like generative AI. By promptly evaluating and integrating these innovations into your operations, you ensure your company remains at the forefront of your industry. As you navigate the potential challenges and constraints that generative AI might present, it is also important to anticipate the significant shifts in your operational models.

      We’re here to help you assess the true impact and identify differentiating, pragmatic opportunities with generative AI across your entire business. Let’s work together to set the course for your future vision as an AI-enabled enterprise and make that vision a reality.

      How We Work 
      With You 

      RAPID ENTERPRISE ASSESSMENT

      Rapid Enterprise Assessment

      We help you quickly uncover opportunities with generative AI for your business to implement. Leveraging your enterprise data, industry and proprietary data sources along with our proprietary transformation platform, we assess the potential impact of generative AI on your business and prioritize strategic use cases to create a roadmap that will drive competitive advantage. 

      GENERATIVE AI WORKSHOP

      Generative AI Workshop

      To kickstart your generative AI innovation pipeline, we leverage the Rapid Enterprise Assessment to facilitate a workshop that helps your team identify unique opportunities to make an impact in the market; define what data you have, where it is stored and how it is used; and recommend a portfolio of AI projects to accelerate the operational use of AI-enabled processes, products and jobs.

      COMPETITIVE DIFFERENTIATOR IDENTIFICATION

      Competitive Differentiator Identification 

      To gain true value, generative AI and its complementary technologies can’t be used right out of the box. You need to have the proper data management, data curation and knowledge management in place to effectively build these technologies into your processes and products to fully operationalize them. We can help you enhance your data capabilities and identify differentiating assets that drive real results.

      STRATEGY & ROADMAP DEVELOPMENT

      Strategy & Roadmap Development

      Using our lighthouse approach, we project the potential impacts of generative AI on your business to help you define a future-state operating model and understand the financial implications. This analysis assists in building a compelling case for change. We then employ a backcasting approach to identify the most effective starting point that can deliver near-term wins for your business and create a comprehensive roadmap to reach your ideal state. This strategic method ensures that your focus is on the most beneficial AI initiatives and that your investments are effectively prioritized.

      ACCELERATED ENGINEERING TEAM OPTIMIZATION

      Accelerated Engineering Team Optimization

      To evolve the roles of your engineers while increasing productivity and accelerating time to market, we help you optimize the organization for growth by building the right balance of AI-enabled human ingenuity. Through our comprehensive onboarding and training program, we quickly equip engineers with the necessary skills and knowledge to overcome the challenges posed by AI-assistance tools and help put productivity metrics in place to prove value.
