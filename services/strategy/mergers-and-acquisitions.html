      Mergers & Acquisitions

      Accelerate investment value realization and chart new opportunities with end-to-end deal support.

      Explore Our Latest Work

      Technology strategy and capital deployment have never been more critical. 

      As software and data reshape the business world, investors are embracing more technology-driven strategies to enable innovation and growth — and with bigger stakes. Our global M&A team takes a comprehensive approach by leveraging vast strategic, industry, operational and technology expertise to advise and execute on even the most complex deals. 

      We partner with organizations from top private equity firms to Fortune 500 companies, enabling them to invest with confidence, recognize the rewards and risks associated with their goals, and drive value quickly throughout the entire transaction process.

      Our Client
      Engagements

      01

      Validating the growth options of a leading digital transformation company

      During a buy-side commercial due diligence of a top digital consulting and engineering company, we confirmed the target’s growth options and business plan with a focus on the key assumptions supporting its top-line growth and track record. As part of our review, we provided the client with potential M&A targets that could spur inorganic opportunities for the target company in a new geography.

      02

      Determining investment feasibility and identifying cost-saving process improvements

      Our product and technology due diligence of a software company in the asset management industry confirmed that its architecture and infrastructure supported the transactional scalability required to meet the investment objectives. We also provided investment recommendations, which ultimately enabled the client to streamline processes, lower costs and speed up product delivery to clients.

      03

      Delivering IT migration plan within six months for carve-out of leading vehicle maker 

      During a global truck manufacturer’s carve-out from its parent company, we developed a comprehensive aftersales retail IT strategy and provided a three-year roadmap for the migration of 30+ applications and systems and all related data.
