      Core &
      Enterprise       Systems

      Fostering transformation and innovation for sustained enterprise growth.

      Contact Us

      Today, enterprise processes and the core systems that support them are extremely complex.

      This is especially true for large companies, where business processes can be vast and intricate. To manage these complexities, modern enterprise applications — designed to handle core processes such as finances, human capital management, supply chain and complex logistics — are more critical than ever before. 

      N & DS’s team of enterprise application experts have functional and technical knowledge of packaged applications, with extensive experience in large-scale product development. We create modular, flexible and scalable solutions that are easily modifiable and extensible, and can be supported for years to come. To provide best-in-class enterprise software solutions that help our clients gain a competitive edge in today's fast-paced business environment, we partner with industry leaders – such as SAP, Microsoft, Salesforce, Workday and Oracle – to bring your vision to life.

      Our Client Solutions

      01

      Driving Decision-Making & Implementation of an ERP System

      We provided a comprehensive assessment of multiple enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems for a food truck network to identify the most suitable solution for lean production and process manufacturing, shortlisting SAP and MS Dynamics and then further implementing MS Dynamics 365 Finance and Operations.

      02

      Empowering Digital Transformation with SAP S/4HANA 

      We partnered with a multinational tobacco company to drive digital transformation through SAP S/4HANA Retail (RISE with SAP), covering corporate template design and its implementation for EU markets as a first step, followed by a global roll-out program with 35+ countries.

      03

      Streamlining Healthcare Operations with MS Dynamics 365 Online Adoption 

      We worked with a biotech company on a complex migration to MS Dynamics 365 Online via MS Fast-track and SSIS, where we helped elaborate its order management process, medical accessioning and integration with laboratory information management systems (LIMS).

      04

      Crafting an Effective Ecommerce Strategy for Customer Success 

      We worked with a well-known drugstore chain to digitize and upscale its eCommerce website to ensure a more streamlined process across all business units and better customer experience due to numerous integrations of Salesforce Commerce, Sales and Marketing Clouds, implementation of Einstein intelligence and development of retail mobile application.

      05

      Optimizing Retail Processes with Next-Generation Cloud Solutions  

      We provided greenfield development of next-generation industry-centric (CPG, retail and distribution, and wholesale) cloud-native solutions for a multinational software corporation in the area of intelligent returns management, revenue growth and unified demand forecast.
