      Innovation starts with a conversation.

      Insightful conversations require specialized know-how. For decades, we’ve designed the future by listening to, and collaborating with, people where they work and live.

      Our experts learn, ideate, build and test seamlessly with partners, end users and key industry opinion leaders through environments, stimuli and protocols tailor-made to help us pick up the small signals that standard voice-of-customer and secondary market research miss.

      We are driven to make the future real.

      Our Core Disciplines

      From early technology feasibility and strategic direction to full-spectrum design, development and commercialization, we work across the stages of product development, tailoring projects to meet your needs.

      Mechanical Engineering

      Mechanical Engineering

      We investigate clients' complex challenges and develop technologies into products by following a rigorous design process that enables optimal user experiences while mitigating technical risk early. We deliver manufacturable, validated physical products through iterative prototyping and deep technical analysis.

      Electrical engineering

      Electrical Engineering

      We develop electrical solutions that power, drive and connect innovative products. Harnessing the newest wireless, IoT and sensor technologies — and a deep understanding of regulatory and safety constraints — we get products out the door and into the most challenging markets.

      industrial design

      Industrial Design

      We engage experiences on an intimate and human scale through form, materials, and interaction. Starting at the touchpoints and scaling outwards, we connect the tangible experience back to the design intent, infusing desire into objects that resonates into the experiences around them.

      human factors engineering

      Human Factors

      We explore, study and design the interactions between people, products and their environments across consumer and regulated medical device industries. Our deep expertise in regulatory guidance documents, standards and industry best practices enables us to drive actionable design insights, control for risks and validate that our designs accommodate their intended users, uses and use environments for both physical and digital products. 

      ux/ui design

      UX/UI Design

      We use technology to create human-product experiences that react and adapt to the needs of the user and device. Interaction design is the real-time manifestation of the relationship users have with brands, physical products and digital interfaces, and is a skill set we use to help companies deliver exceptional experiences.

      manufacturing support

      Manufacturing Support

      We partner with our clients to identify new production technologies, capabilities or vendors, or we work within an established manufacturing environment. We incorporate manufacturing best practices in the development process, informing our decisions based on consumer and business needs.

      IoT & Embedded Engineering

      IoT & Embedded Engineering

      We bring hardware to life with embedded software that meets demanding performance and regulatory requirements while enabling graphical user experiences, IoT communication and device functionality. We achieve high-quality, reliable results through automated testing, peer review, continuous integration and configuration management.

      physical/digital prototyping

      Physical/Digital Prototyping

      Build. Test. Learn.

      We’re all about prototyping. Product, service or experience; we iterate early and often in NDS's Made Real Lab. We are deliberate in our approach to level of fidelity, which is centered around proving value quickly before further investment is made. Our in-house prototyping capabilities accelerate the development process and providing you with the utmost confidence in the chosen direction.

      Quality assurance

      Quality Assurance

      With certifications in, and demonstrated compliance to, key international consensus standards — including ISO 9001, ISO 13485 (Quality Management Systems for Medical Devices) and IEC 62304 (Medical Software Lifecycle) — we help global organizations obtain compliance, on-time delivery and maintain audit-readiness using secure and repeatable processes.

      Innovation & Testing Labs

      NDS’s facilities complement our exceptional personnel and global presence, providing a strong adjunct to our in-field-based learning. Rapid in-situ building, learning and observation are made possible through our specialty labs and tool suites.

      User Research Lab

      On-site observation rooms provide opportunities for contextual learning and product research with representative user groups.

      Made Real Lab

      From early iterative engineering models to small-volume prototype production runs, NDS’s Made Real Lab creates prototypes for every stage in the development process.

      R&D & Reliability Lab

      Equipped with tools to measure and analyze and the space to construct test rigs that evaluate long-term reliability, we run verification studies and perform inspections in compliance with ISO 13485 regulatory standards.

      Human Factors Suite

      Our human factors engineers are equipped with the tools to explore, study and design interactions between people, products and their environments.

      AR/VR 4D Lab

      Our space and design experts utilize a 4D lab to create physical and digital experiences that allow users to immerse themselves in the environments at play.

      Electronics Lab

      From early technical feasibility to PCB bring-up and design verification, our electrical engineering lab provides the calibrated instrumentation, tools and rework facilities necessary to foster rapid progress.
