      AI has changed everything.

      How is your engineering organization changing to embrace it and unleash innovation?

      In today's rapidly changing software product development landscape, there’s more to improving product quality, speeding up time to market, and enhancing productivity, scalability and innovation than technology alone. Your business outcomes depend not only on your GenAI tools, agents and infrastructure, but also your processes and how your teams operate within this AI-infused ecosystem.

      When you orchestrate these components for every part of the SDLC, you can radically optimize development and deliver superior products that align with dynamic market requirements while exceeding customer expectations.

      Our Client Work

      Implementing Full-Scale GenAI Adoption Across the SDLC

      A health management company sought a deep understanding of the true impact of GenAI on the software development process and strategies to enhance engineering team input. We collaborated with our client during a three-month program with comprehensive support, tailored training sessions and real-time monitoring, which spanned multiple product streams and encompassed eight teams of over 100 participants. In this program, we successfully established a GenAI ecosystem, identified GenAI champions, developed training materials and defined a broader-scope improvement program. 

      Validating Productivity Enhancement with AI-Powered EngX

      A telemedicine company wanted to explore GitHub’s Copilot’s potential to enhance engineering team productivity. This investigation encompassed AI integration, training, measurement and potential organizational rollout, all aimed at assessing the tool's capacity to amplify development efficiency, minimize errors and elevate software quality. After conducting our EngX assessment and following our proprietary EngX framework during each stage of the process to seamlessly integrate GitHub Copilot and validate its efficiency, our client decided to expand the tool deployment to the entire organization. We provided our client with a rollout plan, toolset, documentation and EngX best practices essential to scale. 

      Integrating GenAI into a European Automotive OEM's SDLC

      A leading European automotive OEM undertook a transformative journey to fully integrate GenAI throughout its SDLC. Eager to understand GenAI's genuine impact on the software development process and optimize engineering team contributions, the automotive giant collaborated with us in a three-month initiative. During this program, spanning a product stream and involving two teams, we provided robust support, conducted customized training sessions and implemented real-time monitoring. The successful establishment of a GenAI ecosystem within the automotive OEM's operations was achievedluding the creation of tailored training materials and the formulation of a comprehensive improvement program with broader scope. This collaborative initiative has positioned the automotive OEM at the forefront of innovative and efficient software development practices by seamlessly integrating GenAI into the SDLC.

      Transforming to AI-Native Engineering Organization

      N & DS AI/RUN™ is an overarching GenAI-powered methodology that integrates AI into the core of engineering processes, accelerating the SDLC and revolutionizing the delivery of digital products.

      AI/RUN offers a 360-degree approach, combining the best AI and engineering practices to deliver optimal outcomes at the organization, team and individual levels. It focuses on governance, performance tracking, automation and upskilling through AI education while offering an ecosystem of tools and best practices to improve efficiency and innovation in software projects and achieve faster time-to-market at reduced costs. Having this ecosystem enables our clients to choose the best tools for any given task, and quickly customize agents and workflows on top of the existing foundation.
