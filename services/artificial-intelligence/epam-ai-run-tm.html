      Revolutionizing the Delivery of Digital Products 

      Contact us

      Generative AI is changing the world of work — and how we shape products — in waves.

      The first wave showed that AI-powered tools can increase individual productivity, or even set you back if you don’t use them correctly. But the true opportunity lies in transforming how your teams collectively approach product innovation. Only then can you achieve real value.

      This next wave demands a fundamental change in your ways of working… change that supports end-to-end product development processes and your multidisciplinary teams. It requires an ecosystem of AI agents, infrastructure, tools and AI-native processes to optimize complex product development processes.

      Your team members must become evolved experts who can collaboratively, responsibly, securely and creatively harness generative AI (GenAI) — bringing higher quality products to your customers, faster. To assist here, we developed a proven AI-native methodology that orchestrates and enables teams, activating the AI ecosystems — and training — necessary to accelerate and improve development.

      Introducing NDS AI/Run™, a product development methodology powered by AI that supports your multidisciplinary teams as they pioneer the next evolution of product innovation…
